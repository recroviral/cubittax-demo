Meet Our Team

Hardik Patel

Behold, the visionary Founder and Mastermind behind CubitTax. Hardik Patel is a luminary in the realm of fiscal affairs, traversing the intricate landscape of taxation from its very foundations to the zenith of appellate intricacies.
His relentless pursuit of knowledge encompasses the realm of Global Taxation & Laws, where he is currently engrossed in a rigorous pursuit of the esteemed Certified Public Accountant (CPA) qualification.
Hardik boasts a rich tapestry of experience spanning Audit, Investigation, Due Diligence, Taxation (both Direct and Indirect), Company Law, Management Consultancy, Information System Audit, and Trust Registration.
His mastery extends to a plethora of software tools, including Sage, QuickBooks, FreshBooks, SAP, and Ceridian.
Hardik has orchestrated triumphant collaborations with cross-functional teams, steering projects across diverse industries such as finance, real estate, healthcare, and manufacturing in Canada.

Jay Shah

Jay Shah, a Director at CubitTax, stands as a beacon of expertise in the intricacies of departmental operations, ranging from the grassroots to the summit of appellate proceedings.
His accolades include DISA (Diploma in Information System Audit) and FAFD (Forensic Audit and Fraud Detection) qualifications conferred by ICAI, with an ongoing pursuit of DIIT (Diploma in International Taxation) offered by ICAI.
Jay's relentless pursuit of excellence extends to the realm of Global Taxation & Laws, where he is ardently studying for the prestigious Certified Public Accountant (US CPA) qualification.
He brings a wealth of experience in Audit, Investigation, Due Diligence, Taxation (both Direct and Indirect), Company Law, Management Consultancy, Information System Audit, Trust Registration, and RERA.
His proficiency spans a global spectrum, encompassing QuickBooks, Xero, Sage, Tally, Busy, ERP, Miracle, and other globally recognized accounting software.

Aagam Dalal

Aagam Dalal, a Director at CubitTax, stands as the embodiment of excellence. He clinched the coveted All India 2nd Rank in CA Final, May 2018, embellished with four Gold Medals, including a triumph in taxation.
Aagam is also a revered educator, imparting knowledge as a visiting faculty for CA Inter and CA Final at the prestigious institution. He has illuminated the minds of over 80,000 students worldwide with his expertise in Direct Tax and Law.

He serves as an invaluable advisor in corporate restructuring and business capital restructuring matters, as well as navigating the complex terrain of NR taxation.
